# World Cup — "I Took Out a Loan" (90s TikTok / Reel)

**Format:** Voiceover over your own DJI Pocket footage. No talking head.
**Runtime target:** 85–90s.
**Structure:** Doubt → flip → payoff → call to action ("Both, in sequence").
**Money rule:** No personal numbers. The only dollar figures spoken are the generic Morocco breakdown. "Loan" is used as an emotional stake with NO amount attached.
**Tech rule:** One subtle DJI Pocket mention, nothing more.

---

## The script (voiceover + shots)

Read the voiceover conversationally, like you're telling one friend. The on-screen text is for sound-off viewers.

### 0:00–0:04 — HOOK
**VO:** "I took out a loan to watch the World Cup… and everyone told me I was crazy."
**On-screen text:** `I took out a LOAN for this 😳`
**B-roll:** Your loudest, fullest shot — the packed stadium wide, OR you walking up toward it. Save the *biggest* visual for here. Cut on the beat.

### 0:04–0:24 — THE DOUBT (the case against going)
**VO:** "Because here's what everybody says: don't fall for the FOMO. The tickets are insane right now. Just wait — catch the next one somewhere cheaper. Like Morocco."
**VO:** "So I actually ran the numbers. A flight out there? Around fifteen hundred. The ticket itself? Call it five hundred. Then a hotel. Then food. Then getting around for a week."
**On-screen text (stack them in one at a time):**
`Flight ✈️ ~$1,500`
`Ticket 🎟️ ~$500`
`Hotel 🏨 …`
`Food 🍽️ …`
`Getting around 🚆 …`
**B-roll:** Train ride, window scenery, phone in hand. Keep it moving — this is the "thinking out loud" section.

### 0:24–0:42 — THE FLIP (turn the argument)
**VO:** "By the time you add it all up, you're not saving anything. You're spending a fortune to chase it across the world."
**VO:** "But this time? It came to me. The World Cup came to my country — and that almost never happens once in a lifetime."
**On-screen text:** `It came to MY country.`
**B-roll:** Fan-zone / merchant-square vibe — crowds, flags, jerseys, you in the middle of it. Energy rising.

### 0:42–1:08 — THE PAYOFF (the experience)
**VO:** "So I went. Solo. Camera in my pocket — small enough to catch everything."  *(← the DJI line, kept light)*
**VO:** "The fan zone before the match was electric. The train, packed with people from everywhere. And then I walked in…"
**VO:** "I have never felt anything like it in my life. Jam-packed. Loud. Every single person living for that one moment."
**On-screen text:** `Never felt anything like this.`
**B-roll:** This is the emotional peak. Stadium reveal, crowd roar, the wide bowl, your POV walking in. Let one big shot breathe for ~2s with the crowd's natural sound up.

### 1:08–1:28 — RESOLUTION + CTA
**VO:** "So no — I don't regret the loan. Some things you just can't get back."
**VO:** "If the World Cup ever comes to your country, don't let the price talk you out of it. Save up, make the sacrifice, and go live it once. Every other one, you can watch from your couch. This one? You show up."
**On-screen text:** `If it comes to YOU — GO. 🏆`
**B-roll:** Your "coming back" clips — calmer, reflective. End on a strong still or you leaving the stadium.

---

## Editing notes
- **Cut the hook on a beat.** The first frame must be your most striking shot, not a slow build-up.
- **Pace:** fast cuts (1–2s) through the doubt/math section; let the stadium reveal hold longer.
- **Sound:** voiceover sits on top; duck the music under the VO and push the crowd's natural audio up during the payoff for ~2s.
- **Captions:** burn in subtitles for the whole video — most people watch muted.
- **Music:** something building/anthemic that peaks at 0:42 (the flip into the experience).
- You shot solo, so lean into intimate POV framing — it sells the "just me and this moment" feeling.
