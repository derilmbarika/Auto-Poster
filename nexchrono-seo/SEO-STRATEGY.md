**NexChrono is effectively invisible to Google** — 1 ranking keyword, zero estimated traffic. This is not a "recover lost rankings" job; it is a **build-from-zero** job. The implication is decisive:

- We **cannot** win the head terms yet. `rolex` (673K vol), `rolex watches` (74K), `omega watches` (135K), `patek philippe` (165K) are owned by Bob's Watches / Jomashop with thousands of referring domains. Chasing them now wastes effort.
- We **can** win **mid-tail commercial**, **model-specific**, **sell/trade**, and **local Myrtle Beach** queries where authority requirements are low and buyer intent is high. That is the entire near-term game.

**Strategy in one line:** capture high-intent buyers and sellers on long/mid-tail terms with purpose-built, schema-rich category and service pages, build topical authority with a focused blog, and let the local atelier anchor a defensible local pack — then graduate to broader terms as authority compounds.

## 2. Competitor read

- **Bob's Watches** is the category blueprint. It ranks #1 for `rolex submariner` (135K), `pre owned rolex` (14.8K), `rolex air king` (27.1K) and dominates via (a) one strong URL per Rolex model, (b) a deep "watch-resources / rolex-blog" content hub (`rolex serial numbers`, `what is cosc`, `why are rolex so expensive`), and (c) a high-value `sell-rolex-watch` page (`sell my rolex` CPC **$14.85**). **We mirror this architecture, scoped to what we can win.**
- **Jomashop** competes on breadth/price across all brands — not our fight.
- **Crown & Caliber** has collapsed to ~3.6K keywords — proof that a CPO competitor's visibility is *not* guaranteed and there is room to take share.
- **Highest-commercial-value cluster in the niche = "sell/trade"** (CPCs $8–$18). NexChrono already offers Sell/Trade + free appraisal, so this is the single best ROI cluster and is under-built on the current site.

## 4. On-page SEO blueprint (Rank Math)

**Title-tag templates** (≤60 chars, keyword-first, brand suffix):
- Homepage: `Certified Pre-Owned Rolex & Luxury Watches | NexChrono`
- Rolex category: `Pre-Owned Rolex Watches for Sale — Certified | NexChrono`
- Model page: `Used Rolex {Model} for Sale — Certified Pre-Owned | NexChrono`
- Brand page: `Pre-Owned {Brand} Watches — Authenticated | NexChrono`
- Sell hub: `Sell My Rolex — Free Appraisal, Best Price | NexChrono`
- Local page: `Pre-Owned Rolex & Watch Buyer in Myrtle Beach SC | NexChrono`
- Product: `{Year} Rolex {Model} {Ref} — {Dial} | Pre-Owned | NexChrono`

**Meta-description template** (≤155 chars, intent + trust + CTA): *"Shop certified pre-owned {Model} watches, expert-authenticated and guaranteed genuine. Free overnight shipping & low-price guarantee. Browse in-stock now."*

**H1 fix:** homepage H1 is currently the slogan **"Time, Perfected."** — change to a keyworded H1 (`Certified Pre-Owned Rolex & Luxury Watches`) and demote the slogan to a styled tagline/H2. Every category and model page needs a unique, keyword-bearing H1.

**Per-page checklist:** unique H1 with primary kw · 150–400 words of unique intro copy above the fold of the grid (not boilerplate) · FAQ block (3–5 Q&As) targeting cluster questions · internal links to parent + sibling models + the matching sell page · descriptive image ALT (`pre-owned rolex submariner 124060`) · canonical set · breadcrumb.

## 5. Schema / structured data (JSON-LD)
- **Organization + LocalBusiness** (JewelryStore) on homepage/contact with NAP (527 Broadway St, Myrtle Beach SC 29577), geo, hours, `sameAs`, aggregateRating (the BBB A+/5-star) — fuels the local pack.
- **Product + Offer** (price, priceCurrency, itemCondition = `UsedCondition`, availability, brand) on every product — drives rich results & Merchant eligibility.
- **BreadcrumbList** sitewide.
- **FAQPage** on category/sell/local pages and FAQ blocks.
- **Article** on every blog post.
- *(Per project memory: term-meta isn't REST-writable and `<script>` is stripped from term descriptions — inject category/sell schema via the Rank Math schema templates or a head hook, not the term description body. LiteSpeed caches REST GETs, so cache-bust after writes.)*

## 6. Front-end / technical "make it better"
1. **Core Web Vitals** — biggest lever for a luxury-image-heavy WooCommerce site: serve hero/product images as WebP/AVIF, set explicit width/height (kill CLS), lazy-load below-fold, preload the LCP hero. Confirm LiteSpeed Cache image-optim + critical CSS is on.
2. **Mobile-first** — most luxury-watch buying browsing is mobile; verify tap targets, sticky add-to-cart, and that the Elementor homepage doesn't ship desktop-weight assets to phones.
3. **Trust signals above the fold** — surface "Expert-Certified / Guaranteed Genuine," BBB A+, low-price guarantee, free overnight shipping as visible badges near the product grid and on PDPs (conversion + E-E-A-T).
4. **Faceted navigation** — let users filter by model/price/material/condition with clean, indexable URLs for the valuable ones (e.g. `/rolex/submariner/`) and `noindex` thin filter combinations to avoid crawl bloat.
5. **Internal linking** — homepage → top 6 model pages (the "Six Icons" already exist) → each model links to its sell page and back to /rolex/. Add a footer brand/model link block. This is how Bob's Watches funnels equity.
6. **Sitemap & indexation** — submit XML sitemap in GSC, ensure products/categories are indexable, fix any orphan pages, set canonicals on filtered/paginated URLs.
7. **Page experience** — HTTPS (✓), no intrusive interstitials (per memory popups were already killed for Merchant Center — keep them gone), fast TTFB.

## 7. Foundational tasks the owner must do (not doable via code)

## 8. 90-day execution sequence
- **Weeks 1–2 (foundation):** GSC/Bing/GBP setup + sitemap; homepage H1/title/meta fix; Organization/LocalBusiness + Product schema live; CWV image pass.
- **Weeks 3–6 (money pages):** build/optimize the **Sell-Rolex hub + per-model sell pages** (Cluster C — best ROI), the **Myrtle Beach local page** (Cluster D — fastest wins), and the top Rolex model category pages (Submariner, Datejust, Daytona, GMT, Day-Date) with intro copy + FAQ + schema.
- **Weeks 7–12 (authority):** other-brand category pages (Cluster E); launch blog cadence (2–4 posts/mo) starting with `rolex serial number lookup`, `how to spot a fake rolex`, `is a rolex a good investment`, `how much is a rolex`; begin internal-linking + outreach.
- **Ongoing:** monitor in GSC, refresh underperformers, expand winning clusters.

**Expected trajectory:** local + long-tail commercial rankings in 4–10 weeks; meaningful mid-tail (`used rolex submariner`, `sell my rolex`, `rolex daytona for sale`) in 3–6 months as authority builds. Head terms remain a 12-month+ goal contingent on backlinks.
